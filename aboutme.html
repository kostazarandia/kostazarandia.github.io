Short Bio

I am 20 years old, born and raised in the country of Georgia. During my high school studies, I participated in various cultural exchange programs. While being a junior I got a chance to live and experience the United States with the help of the U.S Department of State scholarship. I have been placed in the city of Idaho Falls and went to Idaho Falls High School. Upon return, I contributed to the development of my community by creating an organization that ran in 3 main cities of Georgia and involved up to 4000 high school students. After completing high school studies in the country of Georgia, I was accepted into LCC International University for Contemporary Communication studies. I have professional experience of 3 years at working in different fields such as management, customer support and so on.. Currently, I live in Lithuania and am in my junior year of university. In addition, I hold the position of Resident Assistant in the dormitories of LCC international university and am an HR Intern at The Borgen Project. By living in foreign countries and immersing myself in various cultures, I have come to the realization that my true passion is helping others and seeing them develop by my assistance.
